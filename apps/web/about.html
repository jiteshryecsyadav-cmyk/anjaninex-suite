About Us

Vyapaar Setu is a product of Anjaninex, a technology company based in India. Vyapaar Setu is a cloud-based (SaaS) business-management platform built for Indian textile traders, commission agents (arhat) and small businesses.

Our software helps businesses manage billing and GST invoicing, orders, party outstanding, payments and cheque records, agent commission, full accounting (Trial Balance, Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet) and business reports - all in one secure application.
