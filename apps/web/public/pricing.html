Pricing & Plans

Vyapaar Setu is offered on a subscription (SaaS) basis. Plans are billed monthly or annually depending on the plan chosen at the time of purchase. Applicable GST is charged as per Indian tax law.

How it works

You can start with a free trial. To continue using the service, choose a subscription plan from within the app (Wallet / Plans section) and complete the payment online. Your subscription activates immediately after successful payment.

For current plan prices and features, please contact support@anjaninex.com or view the Plans section inside the app after logging in.
