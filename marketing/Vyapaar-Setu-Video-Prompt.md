## MASTER PROMPT (copy everything below)

Create a high-end, 80-second product marketing video for **"Vyapaar Setu — an Anjaninex product"**, an all-in-one business management app built for Indian textile traders and commission agents (Surat market). The film should feel like a premium Silicon-Valley SaaS launch ad, but rooted in the Indian trade world — aspirational, fast, and trustworthy.

**Visual language:** Cinematic, crisp, modern. Deep purple (#5C1A8B) and gold (#E8B530) brand palette. Soft studio lighting, shallow depth of field, smooth camera moves (dolly-ins, subtle parallax), clean UI mockups floating in 3D space with glossy reflections. Micro-animations on every app screen. 60fps smooth. Aspect ratio 16:9 (also deliver a 9:16 vertical cut).

**Audio:** Confident Hinglish male voiceover (warm, energetic, like a successful young Surat businessman). Uplifting modern Indian-corporate background track with a rising beat; a satisfying "whoosh + click" on each feature reveal.

**On-screen text:** Bold, minimal, English headlines + big numbers. Purple/gold. Appears with kinetic typography, one line at a time.

**Narrative beats (keep each feature on screen 6–8 seconds):**

1. **HOOK (0–8s):** Night. A tired textile trader at his shop desk, buried under paper bills, registers, and a calculator, rubbing his eyes. Quick tense cuts. Text: "Register… GST… udhaari… har raat wahi tension?" VO: "Din bhar mehnat, aur raat ko phir wahi hisaab. Bas — ab aur nahi."

2. **REVEAL (8–15s):** The clutter dissolves into particles that reform into a glowing smartphone showing the Vyapaar Setu dashboard. Logo animates in. Text: "VYAPAAR SETU — poora business, ek app mein." VO: "Milye — Vyapaar Setu. An Anjaninex product."

3. **DASHBOARD (15–23s):** A live dashboard: four KPI cards with animated mini-graphs (Total Sales, Commission, Received, Returns), a rising monthly sales-trend line chart, and a Receivable-vs-Payable chart. Text: "Poore business ki LIVE jhalak." VO: "Ek screen — sales, commission, aur kis party ka kitna baaki. Sab live."

4. **SCAN BILL — the hero moment (23–33s):** A hand photographs a paper bill; instantly the app auto-fills a clean digital bill, and GST fields (CGST, SGST, IGST) populate themselves with a sparkle. Text: "Bill ki photo → 1-click entry. GST auto!" VO: "Bill ki bas photo khींcho — entry apne aap, aur GST khud bat jaata hai. Typing khatam!"

5. **PAYMENTS (33–40s):** Party-wise outstanding list; red 'LATE' tags pulse; one tap opens a report. Text: "Kaun kitna baaki — ek click." VO: "Kis party ne der ki, kaun kitna deta hai — ungli ke ishaare par."

6. **COMMISSION (40–46s):** Agent commission auto-calculating with counting numbers; an agent-wise report slides in. Text: "Agent ka commission — apne aap." VO: "Agent bhaiyon, commission ka poora hisaab — automatic. No manual math."

7. **AI ANJI (46–54s):** A user taps a mic; a friendly AI voice-orb pulses; a Hindi answer bubble appears. Text: "Bolkar poochho — jawab aapki bhasha mein." VO: "'Falane ka balance kitna hai?' — bas poochho. Anji jawab dega, aapki apni bhasha mein."

8. **ACCOUNTS + REPORTS (54–62s):** Trial Balance, P&L, and Balance Sheet generate themselves; an Order-vs-Bill and city-wise buyer report animate. Text: "Poori accounting — auto ready." VO: "Trial Balance, P&L, Balance Sheet — sab apne aap taiyaar."

9. **MONTAGE (62–69s):** Rapid 1-second flashes: Orders, HR live attendance, Bazaar Link marketplace, GST reports, multi-branch switch. Text flashing: "Orders · HR · Bazaar Link · GST · Multi-branch." VO: "Order, staff, bazaar link — poora business, ek jagah."

10. **TRUST + FREE TRIAL (69–75s):** A glowing "15-DIN FREE TRIAL" badge and a "Made in India" tricolor accent. Confident trader smiling, holding phone. VO: "Koi risk nahi — 15 din bilkul free. Pehle chalaao, phir bharosa karo."

11. **END CARD / CTA (75–80s):** Clean purple screen: logo, tagline "Har vyapar ka saathi", website **vyaparsetu.anjaninex.com**, phone **+91 96642 50416**, and a scannable QR code. VO: "Har vyapar ka saathi — Vyapaar Setu. Aaj hi digital ho jaaiye!" Music resolves triumphantly.

**Must-hit wow moments (emphasize with the biggest visual pop):** (a) photographing a bill that auto-fills with automatic GST, (b) the voice AI answering in Hindi, (c) auto-generated financial statements, (d) the "15-day free trial" close.

**Do:** keep it fast, premium, optimistic, and simple. **Avoid:** clutter, tiny text, more than one headline at a time, robotic narration.
