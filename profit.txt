Add-on cross-sell automation

Annual contract for Enterprise

6 months minimum lock-in
Saves churn, reduces customer support thrash


Migration credits (steal competitors)

Tally / Busy user ko 3 months free migration period

WhatsApp marketing (50% lower CAC than Google)

Hindi/Hinglish bulk campaigns

Margin killers to avoid:
❌ Free trials too long (60+ days) — burn cash without conversion
❌ Free unlimited AI — Gemini cost balloons
❌ 24/7 phone support on Starter — eats margin
❌ Custom features for one customer — kills team time
❌ Aggressive discounting — devalues product
